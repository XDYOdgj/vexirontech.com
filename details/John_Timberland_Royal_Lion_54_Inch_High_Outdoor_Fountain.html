Freestanding Outdoor Fountain with Lion Head Water Feature

Elevate your outdoor space with this stunning freestanding fountain from John Timberland. The realistic faux stone finish gives it a rustic and timeless appeal, seamlessly blending with traditional or Tuscan-style gardens. Four gracefully sculpted lion head spouts create a dramatic, cascading water display that brings a soothing, relaxing ambiance to your surroundings. Whether you're looking to add visual interest or enjoy the calming sound of flowing water, this fountain delivers both elegance and serenity to your outdoor living area.

This large Italian-style outdoor fountain is the perfect addition to your backyard or porch deck, turning your outdoor area into a serene and peaceful retreat. With its robust yet sleek design, it can be placed in a garden, on a patio, or near your favorite seating area to create a tranquil water feature. Lightweight and easy to assemble, this fountain is ideal for homeowners who want to enhance their outdoor decor without the hassle of complicated installation.
