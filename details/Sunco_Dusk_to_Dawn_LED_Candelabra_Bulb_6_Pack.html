Sunco's Dusk to Dawn LED Candelabra Bulbs

Light up your space automatically with Sunco's dusk to dawn LED candelabra bulbs. Featuring a reliable photocell sensor, these bulbs activate at sunset and turn off at sunrise, ensuring your outdoor areas are always illuminated when needed without any hassle.

With a 5W=40W power rating, they deliver adequate brightness while maintaining energy efficiency, making them perfect for use in garden, deck, or patio lighting. The 2700K soft white light combined with a frosted glass finish creates a warm and inviting glow that enhances both the aesthetics and functionality of your outdoor decor.

These E12 base bulbs are built to fit seamlessly into sconces and other outdoor fixtures. UL and Title 20 certified, they are safe to use and built for long lasting performance. Enjoy the convenience, style, and reliability of these outdoor lighting bulbs with a 5-year warranty that reflects Sunco's dedication to quality and customer care.
