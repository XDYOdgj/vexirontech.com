This makes them an eco-friendly choice that helps reduce electricity bills without compromising on brightness or style.

Light That Speaks to Your Space

With a cool white light output at 4000K and 550 lumens, these bulbs create a fresh and inviting atmosphere. Whether you're looking to illuminate a bedroom, bathroom, or any other room, their soft yet vibrant glow ensures a welcoming and modern ambiance. The high CRI90 rating means colors look true and rich, perfect for displays, art, and everyday use.

Built to Last, Designed for Performance

These LED candelabra bulbs are built for longevity, offering a long lifespan of up to 25,000 hours. This reduces the frequency of replacements and saves you both time and money in the long run. This makes them perfect for adjustable lighting needs across your home.
