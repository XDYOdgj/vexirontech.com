CREPOW RGB Underwater Pond Lights

Transform your garden, pool, or pond into a stunning aquatic display with these durable and versatile lights. Designed for both indoor and outdoor use, CREPOW offers a perfect blend of style and safety with its IP68 waterproof rating, ensuring worry-free installation in any water feature.

These lights feature 18 high-brightness LED beads paired with a convex lens to create an even spread of illumination. Whether you're looking for a festive glow for holidays or a serene atmosphere for relaxation, the 16 million color options and multiple scene modes allow you to customize your lighting experience with ease.

With Bluetooth APP control and a 130ft remote, you can manage the lights from anywhere, making it simple to adjust colors, brightness, and settings. The adjustable timer and music sync function add an extra layer of convenience, allowing the lights to respond to your favorite tunes or follow a schedule for a hands-free look.

CREPOW delivers a set of 10 high-quality pond lights that can be submerged completely or used above water for ambient effects. Their low-voltage DC 5V output is safe for people and aquatic life, making them an ideal choice for both residential and decorative lighting solutions.
